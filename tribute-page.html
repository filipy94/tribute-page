Great deeds of Sauron

  1. First Age
    • Greatest of Morgorth's servants.
    • Commander of Angband's Fortress
    • Conquered the elvish isle of Tol Sirion
    • Hided from Morgorth's wrath
  2. Second Age
    • Established in the land of Mordor.
    • Corrupted elves to forge the Rings of Power while he forges the One Ring in secret.
    • The second age ends during the battle of the last alliance versus forces of Mordor, when king Isildur cuts the One Ring from Sauron's finger.
  3. Third Age
    • Sauron wasn't dead, but he lost his physical form, hiding in Dol Guldur's stronghold assuming the name of Necromancer of Dol Guldur while he looking the One Ring back.
    • Valar send five istaris, including Gandalf the grey to oppose Sauron.
    • The hobbit Smeagol found the One Ring in 2460 year of third age.
    • 2850 - Galdalf discovered that the Necromancer of Dol Guldur is Sauron.
    • 3019 - The War of the Ring - Frodo Baggins destroy the One Ring in Mount Doom killing Sauron permanently and saving Middle-Earth.
